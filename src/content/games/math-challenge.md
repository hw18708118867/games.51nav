## About the Math Challenge

Mental arithmetic is a fundamental cognitive skill that we use every day, often without realizing it. From splitting a restaurant bill to calculating a discount, quick mental math saves time and builds confidence with numbers.

### The Cognitive Benefits

Mental math exercises multiple cognitive systems simultaneously: working memory (holding numbers in mind), executive function (choosing calculation strategies), and processing speed. Regular practice strengthens these cognitive pathways.

### How Our Challenge Works

You have 30 seconds to solve as many arithmetic problems as possible. Problems include addition, subtraction, and multiplication. As your score increases, the problems get harder — larger numbers, more complex operations. Your streak of correct answers multiplies your points, rewarding both speed and accuracy.

### Tips for Improvement

The key to faster mental math is building a repertoire of strategies. Rather than solving every problem the same way, learn to recognize patterns and choose the most efficient approach. With regular practice, these strategies become automatic, and your speed will naturally increase.
