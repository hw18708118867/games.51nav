## About Tank Battle

Tank battle games have been a staple of video gaming since the 1980s, from the classic Tank 1990 (Battle City) on the NES to modern multiplayer tank games. The top-down perspective, destructible environments, and strategic combat create a uniquely engaging gameplay loop.

### Strategic Combat

Tank Battle combines reflexes with strategy. The destructible walls create a dynamic battlefield — you can destroy cover to create new angles of attack, or preserve walls to protect yourself from enemy fire. Each wave brings more enemies, requiring you to adapt your strategy.
