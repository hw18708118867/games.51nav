## About Connect Four

Connect Four was invented in 1974 by Howard Wexler and Ned Strong. Originally marketed by Milton Bradley, it became one of the best-selling board games of all time. The game's simple rules — drop discs, connect four — hide surprising strategic depth.

### Mathematical Depth

Connect Four was mathematically solved in 1988: with perfect play, the first player can force a win. This makes it a rich subject for game theory and AI research. The branching factor is much larger than Tic-Tac-Toe, making it a better challenge for both humans and computers.
