## About 2048

2048 was created in 2014 by 19-year-old Italian developer Gabriele Cirulli as a weekend project. It became a viral sensation, with millions of players worldwide. The game's simple mechanics — slide tiles, merge numbers — create surprisingly deep strategy.

### The Addictive Formula

2048 combines the satisfaction of number merging with the strategic depth of a puzzle game. Each move changes the entire board state, requiring constant adaptation. The game is easy to learn but incredibly difficult to master, with players spending hours trying to reach the elusive 2048 tile.

### Strategic Depth

Despite its simplicity, 2048 has a rich strategic layer. Corner strategies, snake patterns, and board management all play a role in achieving high scores. The game exercises spatial reasoning, planning, and pattern recognition — making it excellent brain training disguised as entertainment.
