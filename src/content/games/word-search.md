## About Word Search

Word Search puzzles have been a beloved pastime for generations. The simple premise — find hidden words in a grid of letters — belies the cognitive engagement required to scan, recognize patterns, and filter out irrelevant information.

### The Cognitive Benefits

Word searches train visual scanning, pattern recognition, and selective attention. You must systematically search the grid while ignoring distractions — a skill that transfers to many real-world tasks like proofreading, data analysis, and even navigating crowded spaces.

### How Our Puzzle Works

Each puzzle contains 6 themed words hidden in a 10×10 grid. Words can appear in any of the 8 compass directions. Click and drag to select letters — the game highlights your selection in real-time. Found words are automatically crossed off the list, and the letters are highlighted in green.
