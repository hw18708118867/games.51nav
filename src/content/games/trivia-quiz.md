## About Trivia Quiz

Trivia has been a popular form of entertainment for centuries, from Victorian parlor games to modern pub quizzes and TV game shows. The appeal is universal: testing your knowledge against others in a fun, low-stakes format.

### Cognitive Benefits

Regular trivia practice exercises memory recall, expands general knowledge, and improves the ability to retrieve information quickly. The variety of topics keeps the brain engaged across multiple knowledge domains, making it a well-rounded cognitive exercise.
