## About Snake

Snake is one of the most iconic video games of all time. Originally created in 1976 as "Blockade," it gained worldwide fame when Nokia included it on their mobile phones in 1998. Over 400 million Nokia phones shipped with Snake, making it one of the most played games in history.

### Simple Yet Addictive

The premise is deceptively simple: move a growing snake around a grid, eating food while avoiding collisions. This creates a natural tension — the longer you play, the more constrained your movement becomes. The game exercises spatial planning, quick decision-making, and hand-eye coordination.
