## About Maze Runner

Mazes have fascinated humans for thousands of years, from the legendary Labyrinth of Crete to the hedge mazes of European palaces. Maze-solving exercises spatial navigation, planning, and visual-spatial memory — all key cognitive skills.

### DFS Maze Generation

Our mazes are generated using a randomized depth-first search algorithm. This produces "perfect" mazes — mazes with exactly one path between any two points. The result is a maze that's always solvable but never trivial, with winding corridors that challenge your spatial reasoning.
