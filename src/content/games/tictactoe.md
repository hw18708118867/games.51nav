## About Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe (also known as Noughts and Crosses) is one of the oldest and most universal games. Evidence of similar games dates back to ancient Egypt and the Roman Empire. The game's simplicity — a 3×3 grid, two players, three in a row — belies its importance in game theory and AI research.

### A Solved Game

Tic-Tac-Toe is a "solved game" — with perfect play from both sides, it always ends in a draw. This makes it an ideal teaching tool for game theory, minimax algorithms, and basic AI concepts. In fact, one of the first AI programs ever written was a Tic-Tac-Toe player.
