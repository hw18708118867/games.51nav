## About Racing

Obstacle avoidance games test the core of human reflexes: perceive a threat, react quickly, avoid it. The simple left-right movement belies the cognitive demands of tracking multiple threats, predicting their trajectories, and executing precise movements under increasing time pressure.

### Progressive Difficulty

Our Racing game uses a carefully tuned difficulty curve. Speed starts slow and increases linearly over time. Obstacle spawn rates also increase, creating a natural progression from easy to challenging. This mirrors the flow state sought in game design — always challenging but never overwhelming.
