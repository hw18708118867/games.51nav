## About Wordle

Wordle took the world by storm in late 2021. Created by software engineer Josh Wardle as a gift for his partner, the simple word-guessing game became a global phenomenon, eventually being acquired by The New York Times.

### The Appeal

Wordle's genius lies in its constraints: one puzzle per day, six guesses, five letters. These limits create a perfect daily ritual — short enough to do over coffee, challenging enough to feel rewarding. The color-coded feedback system is instantly intuitive, and the shareable results (without spoilers) fueled its viral spread on social media.

### Strategic Vocabulary Building

Beyond entertainment, Wordle is excellent vocabulary training. Regular play expands your active vocabulary, improves pattern recognition for letter combinations, and develops systematic problem-solving skills. The game exercises the same cognitive systems used in word puzzles, crosswords, and language learning.
