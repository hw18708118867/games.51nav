## About Minesweeper

Minesweeper is one of the most iconic computer games of all time. Originally bundled with Microsoft Windows, it introduced millions of people to the joy of logical deduction games.

### A Brief History

Minesweeper was first included with the Microsoft Entertainment Pack in 1990 and became a standard Windows feature from Windows 3.1 through Windows 7. The game's simple rules but deep strategic gameplay made it an instant classic that has stood the test of time.

### The Logic Behind the Game

Minesweeper is a pure logic puzzle disguised as a game. Each number you reveal constrains where mines can be, and by combining multiple constraints, you can deduce the exact location of every mine. The game exercises deductive reasoning, pattern recognition, and sometimes probabilistic thinking.

### Why Play Minesweeper?

Beyond nostalgia, Minesweeper is excellent brain training. It develops logical deduction skills, pattern recognition abilities, and the capacity to hold multiple constraints in working memory simultaneously. These are the same cognitive skills used in programming, mathematics, and strategic planning.
