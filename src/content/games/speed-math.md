## About Speed Math

Speed Math takes mental arithmetic to the next level. With a full 60 seconds on the clock, all four operations, and a combo scoring system, this challenge pushes both speed and accuracy.

### The Combo System

What makes Speed Math unique is the combo multiplier. Each consecutive correct answer increases your combo, multiplying the points you earn per problem. But one wrong answer resets your combo to zero. This creates a risk-reward dynamic: do you slow down to maintain accuracy, or push for speed at the risk of breaking your streak?

### Bonus Time

Reaching 500 points awards bonus time, keeping the game going. This mechanic rewards players who can maintain high accuracy at speed — it's not just about going fast, but about going fast AND being right.
