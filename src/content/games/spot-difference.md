## About Spot the Difference

Spot the Difference puzzles have been popular for centuries, appearing in newspapers, magazines, and puzzle books. The format is simple but engaging: two images side by side with subtle differences to find. Our digital version uses emoji pairs that are visually similar but distinct.

### The Cognitive Challenge

Finding differences exercises visual scanning, selective attention, and pattern recognition. Your brain must systematically compare many similar items to find the one that doesn't match. This skill transfers to real-world tasks like proofreading, quality control, and visual inspection.
