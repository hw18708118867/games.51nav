## About Ball Sort

Ball Sort puzzles are a modern casual gaming phenomenon. The simple mechanic — pick up a ball, place it on a matching color — is instantly satisfying. The challenge comes from the limited number of tubes and the strategic use of empty space.

### A Relaxing Challenge

Ball Sort sits in the sweet spot between relaxing and challenging. There's no time pressure, no lives to lose — just a puzzle to solve at your own pace. This makes it ideal for short breaks, commuting, or winding down. The progressive difficulty of finding the optimal solution keeps players coming back.
